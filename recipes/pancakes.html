Pancakes

Description

Fluffy and light pancakes that are perfect for breakfast or brunch. Serve with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot griddle and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
