Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food. It’s layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melty mozzarella.

Perfect for family dinners and make-ahead meals!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onions, Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef with garlic and onion.
  3. Add tomato sauce and seasoning to the beef.
  4. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
