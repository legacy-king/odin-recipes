Salad

Description

A refreshing salad made with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a light vinaigrette dressing. Perfect as a side dish or a light meal!

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens.
  2. Slice the cherry tomatoes and cucumbers.
  3. Thinly slice the red onion.
  4. In a large bowl, combine all the vegetables.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, then season with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.
